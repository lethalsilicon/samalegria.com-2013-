Arduino

Open-Source Electronics Prototyping

Goal: I want to teach children and adults how the electronics in the world around them work. The Arduino is good way not only how to teach physical electronics, but also show the power of a microcontroller and what is possible. I've been playing around with the Arduino and how to use them as a teaching tool for kids.

Note: For anyone with keen eyes, you may have noticed that my portfolio's color scheme is an homage to the Arduino packaging. To view the amazing work done by the designer, visit http://todo.to.it.
